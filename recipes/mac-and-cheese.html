Mac and Cheese

Description:

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Boil the noodles, drain and transfer to a prepared bnaking dish
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the nooodles and stir
  3. Make the topping, spread it over macararoni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown
